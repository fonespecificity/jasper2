IPL 2018 Auction Completed List Of Players

IPL 2018 auction has concluded yesterday after going on for 2 days in Bangalore, India. A big pool of 580 players went under the hammer. Total 169 players were sold for a total amount of 4,31,70,000 INR.

While most of the players are from previous season but there are new faces as well. Most expensive player of this season is Ben Stokes for an whopping amount of 12,50,00,000 INR and the most expensive Indian player is Jaydev Undakat who is auctioned for 11,50,00,000 INR.
