Cape Cobra vs Dolphins 2nd Semi-Final Momentum Cup Dream11 Fantasy11 Preview

Welcome to the 2nd semi-final of Momentum One Day Cup 2017-18. This ground is high scoring ground. We will be providing match predictions, probable XI, tips and some tricks to help you win big in this match.

Both the teams are meeting head to head for the 3rd time in this tournament. Dolphins had won both of them leaving Cobra team thirsty for a win. Cape Cobras will be aiming to end the stranglehold the Dolphins have held over them this season when they meet in the second Momentum One-Day Cup semi-final at PPC Newlands in Cape Town today.
